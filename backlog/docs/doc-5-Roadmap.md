# 🗺️ BroodBrain Roadmap 2026-2027+

[Skip to Chinese Version / 跳转到中文版](#中文版-chinese-translation)

---

## 🌈 Vision-Driven: Light of Humanity
> **Chain of Thought**: Individual -> Freedom & Humanity -> Mycelium Protocol Collaboration -> Economic Model Exploration -> Community & Diversity

We believe the future will be defined by a new organizational paradigm: the emergence of **Loose Communities** and **Extreme Individuals**.
- **What does a community need most?** Onboarding + Positive Feedback + Check = **COS72** (Community OS).
- **What do individuals need?** Time, space, economic sustainability, and the support to release their imagination, creativity, and emotions.

---

## 🌱 Phase 1: Genesis Launch (2026.03.30)
**Focus: Cold Start & Infrastructure**
- [ ] **Cos72 Chrome Extension**: User gateway integrating payments and accounts.
- [ ] **SuperPaymaster**: Decentralized Gas Payment Protocol.
- [ ] **AirAccount**: Solving crypto account friction (Social Login).
- [ ] **MushroomDAO**: Launching GToken sale and initial governance vote.

## 🍄 Phase 2: Community Expansion (2026 H2)
**Focus: Collaborative Networks & Hardware Solutions**
- [ ] **Cos72 Community OS**: Launching MyTask, MyShop, and MyVote modules.
- [ ] **xPNTs/MySBT**: Refining reputation and identity protocols.
- [ ] **AirAccount Hardware**: Launching Edge Computing KMS solutions (Overseas/Domestic).
- [ ] **OpenNest**: Incubating Zu.Coffee, HHH, and Arcadia.

## 🌳 Phase 3: Ecosystem Maturity (2027+)
**Focus: Diversity & Sustainability**
- [ ] **Spores/Park Protocol**: Viral growth and Sustainable Public Goods protocols.
- [ ] **OpenCity**: ChiangMai Connect pilot for digital nomads.
- [ ] **DApp Ecosystem**: Comprehensive applications like TradeStar and CoinJar.

## 🌈 愿景驱动：人性之光
> **思维链条**：个体 -> 自由与人性 -> 菌丝协议协作 -> 经济模型探索 -> 社区与多样性

我们相信，未来的组织形态是**松散社区与极致个体**。
